## What is Semaphore?

Semaphore is a data-aware, high performing **micro-service orchestrator**.
Create advanced data flows and expose them through multiple protocols such as HTTP, GraphQL, and gRPC.
Semaphore is designed to adapt to your environment.
Use your existing schema definitions (OpenAPI3, Protobuffers) to interact with your existing services.

  with your existing system(s). Define flows through

## Getting started

The simplest way of getting started is by downloading one of the prebuild <Link to="installation/cli">Semaphore binaries</Link>.
There are various examples available inside the [git repository](https://github.com/jexia/semaphore/tree/master/examples).
Feel free to clone the repository and start playing with the available examples.

If you have any questions feel free to open a new [issue on Github](https://github.com/jexia/semaphore/issues) or to reach out to the community on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/qWByeWG).
